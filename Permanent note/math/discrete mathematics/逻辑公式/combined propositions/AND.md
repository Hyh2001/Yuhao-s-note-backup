
### AND（&&）

$T \quad T \to T$
$T \quad F \to F$
$F \quad T \to F$
$F \quad F \to F$

#### notation

$P \land Q$

#### calculation

1. $A$ AND $B$ = $B$ AND $A$
2. ($A$ AND $B$)AND $C$ = $A$ AND ($B$ AND $C$)
3. $A$ AND ($B$ OR $C$) = ($A$ AND $B$) OR ($A$ AND $C$)
4. $A$ OR ($B$ AND $C$) =  ($A$ OR $B$) AND ($A$ OR $C$)
