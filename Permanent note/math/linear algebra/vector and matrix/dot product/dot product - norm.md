[[dot product]] of a vector with itself equals to its [[norm]]. 
$U \cdot U = ||U||^2$

also using norm and dot product we can proof the following theorem: 

$U \cdot V \leq ||U||||V|| \leq \frac{1}{2}||U||^2+\frac{1}{2}||V||^2$
