For a [[polynomial]] $p_{n}(x)=a_{n}x^{n}+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+\ldots +a_{1}x+a_{0}$, we can rewrite them recursively as
**$v_{0}=a_{n}$
$v_{1}=v_{0}x+a_{n-1}$
$\dots$
$v_{n}=v_{n-1}x+a_{0}=p_{n}(x)$**
