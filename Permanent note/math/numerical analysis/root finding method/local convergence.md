An [[iterative method]] is said to converge locally if there exists a number $\delta > 0$ such that the iterative formula 
$x_{k+1}=\varphi(x_{k})$ is [[convergent]] for every initial guess $x_{0}$ with **$|x_{0} - x^{*}|\leq \delta$.**
