Physical crosslinking is the formation of a bond between polymer chains through **weak interactions**.
It has several properties: 
1. reversible 
2. easy preparation 
3. mild condition
4. do not require toxic reagent 
5. biocompatible 
disadvantages: 
1. low mechanical strength 
2. less stable
