The calculation of a [[cross product]] $u \times v$ actually can be achieved by using [[determinant]]. 
$u \times v = det(\begin{bmatrix}i & j& k\\u_1&u_2&u_3\\v_1&v_2&v_3\end{bmatrix})$ where $i = (1,0,0)$, $j=(0,1,0)$ and $k=(0,0,1)$.
